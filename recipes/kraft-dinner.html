K.D. Mac N' Cheese

Description

K.D. is popular amongst college students for its low cost, ease of preparation, and delicious flavour. All you need is hot water and a bowl!

Ingredients

  • water
  • Box of K.D.

Steps

  1. Pour noodles into a bowl or pot.
  2. Add water.
  3. Place bowl into microwave, or pot onto stove.
  4. Cook until pasta is al dente.
  5. Drain water from pasta.
  6. Add cheese powder and stir.
  7. Enjoy!
